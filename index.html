About

BandSim is an indie game project centered on band growth and live performance. You build your lineup, step onto the stage, and fight for the room one song at a time.

The project is in an early development phase. More updates and playable details will be shared as development progresses.

Core Pillars

Band Growth

Form your band, shape member combinations, and define a path that feels earned instead of scripted.

Live Performance

Build momentum through set flow, stage decisions, and on-the-fly choices that change how a night unfolds.

Atmosphere & Audience

Capture the emotional swing of a livehouse: shifting crowd energy, room pressure, and the thrill of turning a show around.

Development Status

BandSim is currently in active development and remains at an early stage. We are building the foundation carefully and will publish progress updates step by step.
